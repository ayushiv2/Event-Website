Events

To enhance one's skills and make learning more amusing and competitive, LEAD organizes a 3-day long ,its flagship event SHOONYA 4.0 comprising 3 mind bening exhillartaing events. Our flagship event tests critical thinking skills, enrich the competitive nature and boosts the teamwork spirit of the participants

CODEBIZ

6th February

CODEBIZ, an online coding contest, will test participants' ability to solve complex problems most efficiently and creatively.



×
1.CODEBIZ, a 1-and-a-half-hour individual event, is an online coding contest that LEAD will conduct on 6th February 2023 from 9:30 PM onwards.
2.It is open for all intra-college, individual events.
3.Participants must answer three questions of varying difficulties, providing optimal solutions to every question.
4.The contest is open to all years of students.
5.The organizing Team will decide the winner based on accuracy, the number of questions solved, and the time taken to complete.
6.All participants must abide by the server's code of conduct and uphold its decorum. Any infringement of the rules mentioned above will lead to disqualification at the discretion of the organizing Team.
7.Hence all participants should join our official discord server and follow our Instagram page so you can receive all the updates well in time!
8.Each question is allotted a predetermined score and contains various test cases. The organizing Team will give scores based on the number of test cases passed.
9.If several participants have an equal score, the organizing team will give points based on the total time to solve the problems.

QUIKSTER

6th February

QUIKSTER is a quizzing contest challenging participants' knowledge from diverse fields in time-based buzzer rounds.



×
1.QUIKSTER offline quizzing event that will take place on 6th Feb 2023, Saturday, from 4:30 PM onwards.
2.It's a fun event testing the team's knowledge on varied fields, including buzzer round, rapid fire round and much more.
3.The event will be of 5 rounds comprising multiple domains.
4.It's an intra-college event which requires registrations in a team of 2-3 members. Inter-year teams are also allowed in the event.
5.Only the team leader is allowed to fill out the registration form.
6.The leaderboard will be dynamic; teams' rankings will keep changing depending on the points earned.
7.In case of any discrepancy or conflict, the organizing team's decision will be final and binding.
8.Every correct answer will be worth 10 points, and upon failure to answer a question, the organizing team will give the next team in line to answer; the correct answer will yield 5 bonus points.
9.Many rounds will contain negative points, too, for incorrect answers.

4 Block 8

7th February

The final event, 4 BLOCK 8, an exhilarating game of enhanced monopoly, will force the teams to use their wits and place their every move strategically.



×
1. 4 BLOCK 8 is an offline monopoly event that LEAD will conduct on 7th February 2023 from 4:30 PM onwards.
2.Each team should consist of 3-4 members only.
3.Inter-year teams are also allowed in the event.
4.Only the team leader is allowed to fill out the registration form.
5.The event will contain basic concepts of the game Monopoly, namely Community Chests, Chances, and acquiring the cities, with a blend of technical questions.
6.All the teams will be required to be spontaneous in answering the questions to move forward in the game.
7.The leaderboard will be dynamic; teams' rankings will keep changing depending on the points earned.
8.It's an intra-college event that requires registrations in a team only.
9.In case of any discrepancy or conflict, the Organizing Team's decision will be final and binding.
